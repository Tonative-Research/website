Chinenye is a software engineer and researcher dedicated to bridging the digital divide for African languages. As the Head of Engineering at Tonative Research, she leads the development of open-source infrastructure for underrepresented languages like Igbo.

Her work focuses on human-AI collaboration, building scalable datasets and validation tools that ensure native speakers are central to the AI development process. Beyond language technology, she is an active cybersecurity researcher and maintains a technical blog, "UnDebugged Logs," where she documents her insights on infrastructure, automation, and digital security
