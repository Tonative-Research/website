Sharon Ibejih is a Senior Data Scientist and African language AI researcher who has spent the last several years building production-grade machine learning systems across finance, energy, and social impact sectors. She founded Tonative based on her long-standing focus on ensuring African languages are meaningfully represented in modern AI systems.

Alongside her industry work, Sharon has served as an Adjunct Lecturer at Pan-Atlantic University, Nigeria, where she taught data science and natural language processing at the graduate level. Her research sits at the intersection of language technology and responsible AI. She has contributed to peer-reviewed research on speech processing, machine translation, and African language datasets, and has worked with organisations such as Sunbird AI and Data Science Nigeria on open-source tools and large-scale language data curation.
